Science

Research Focus

Current research examines microglial function in neurodegenerative contexts, with specific attention to extracellular vesicle-mediated signaling and tRNA-derived fragment regulation. Work includes analysis of RNA-derived EVs released by microglia cells under inflammatory conditions and characterization of tRNA fragment populations in neurodegenerative disease models.

Research Aims

  • Characterize microglial extracellular vesicle cargo in response to inflammatory stimuli
  • Identify tRNA-derived fragments enriched in microglia-derived EVs
  • Examine functional roles of specific tRF species in neuroinflammatory pathways
  • Develop methodologies for isolating and analyzing small RNA populations from extracellular vesicles

Laboratory Skills

  • Primary cell culture techniques (microglia isolation and maintenance)
  • Extracellular vesicle isolation and characterization
  • RNA extraction, quality assessment, and library preparation
  • Quantitative PCR and cytotoxicity assays
  • Microscopy techniques for cellular imaging
  • Western blotting for protein analysis

Bioinformatics

  • NGS data analysis for small RNA sequencing
  • tRNA fragment identification and annotation
  • Differential expression analysis using statistical frameworks
  • Python and R for data processing and visualization
  • Linux command-line proficiency for pipeline execution

02

Apiary

Mission

Ege Arıcılık operates on principles of scientific beekeeping, emphasizing colony health monitoring, evidence-based husbandry practices, and minimal intervention approaches. Production methods prioritize bee welfare and natural foraging behaviors while maintaining productivity through selective breeding and disease management protocols.

Product Standards

Honey production follows region-specific botanical profiles from distinct geographic zones. Each batch undergoes moisture content verification, pollen analysis for botanical origin confirmation, and absence testing for synthetic compounds. Products are traceable to specific apiary locations and harvesting periods.

Sustainability Practices

  • Colony health monitoring using standardized assessment protocols
  • Integrated pest management without synthetic chemical treatments
  • Maintenance of genetic diversity through selective breeding programs
  • Preservation of forage habitat through partnerships with local landowners
  • Seasonal management aligned with regional flora phenology

Scientific Approach

Operations integrate current research on colony nutrition, pathogen dynamics, and environmental stressors. Management decisions are informed by temperature monitoring, brood pattern assessment, and forage availability data. Record-keeping systems track colony performance metrics across seasons to optimize hive placement and resource allocation.

Sport

Training Philosophy

Training follows a hybrid athlete model integrating resistance training, endurance running, swimming, and mobility work. Programming emphasizes concurrent adaptation across strength and aerobic capacities while managing interference effects through periodization and recovery protocols.

Performance Metrics

  • 10K running performances: multiple races completed at approximately 46-minute pace
  • Resistance training: compound movement progressions tracked across mesocycles
  • Swimming: technique development and distance capacity building
  • Mobility: range of motion assessments and flexibility maintenance

Training Aims

  • Optimize concurrent training adaptations without compromising either strength or endurance gains
  • Improve 10K running time through structured interval training and base building phases
  • Develop swimming efficiency and increase sustainable distance capacity
  • Maintain joint health and functional mobility across all movement patterns
  • Apply evidence-based recovery strategies including sleep optimization and nutrition timing

Research Interest Areas

  • Interference effect mechanisms in concurrent training
  • Periodization models for hybrid athlete development
  • Nutritional strategies for optimizing body composition and performance
  • Recovery modalities and their physiological effects
  • Supplementation efficacy in trained populations
  • Biomechanical analysis of running economy

About

Completed Bachelor of Science in Molecular Biology and Genetics at Izmir Biomedicine and Genome Center, August 2023. Currently pursuing Master of Science at the same institution with research focus on microglia-derived extracellular vesicles and tRNA-derived fragments in neuroinflammatory processes.

Master's thesis examines tRNA fragment populations in microglial extracellular vesicles under inflammatory conditions, utilizing small RNA sequencing, bioinformatics analysis, and functional validation approaches. Research includes both computational analysis and wet laboratory techniques.

Founded Ege Arıcılık, operating multiple apiaries with focus on scientific beekeeping methods, colony health monitoring, and region-specific honey production. Management practices integrate current research on bee nutrition, pathogen control, and sustainable apiculture.

Training follows hybrid athlete programming with structured periodization across strength training, distance running, swimming, and mobility work. Completed multiple 10K races with times around 46 minutes. Approach incorporates exercise physiology literature and evidence-based recovery protocols.

Active in scientific communication through content creation on research methodologies, data analysis approaches, and training science. Interests span neuroscience, immunology, extracellular vesicle biology, and exercise physiology.
